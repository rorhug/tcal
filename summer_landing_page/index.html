What is Tcal?

  • Tcal is not another phone app.
  • Tcal is a service which makes your timetable available in Google Calendar.
  • Tcal keeps time and module changes in sync.

What's the point?

  • Your iPhone or Android comes with a great calendar app. Tcal makes use of it.
  • Know what you have and where to go, without even unlocking your phone.
  • Manage the rest of your busy, personal day/night schedule in your calendar alongside your college timetable.
